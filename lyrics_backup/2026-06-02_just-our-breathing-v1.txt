[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Whispered] (Leave the fading porch light burning low)
I just want to pull you close to me
Let the busy world outside us go
Tonight is right where I am supposed to be

[Verse 1]
Dusty boots and shadows on the floor
I can feel the heat across the room
Every time you inch a little near
I get tangled in your sweet perfume

[Pre-Chorus]
The clock is quiet, but my heart is loud
I'm leaning in to catch your every move
There's a heavy magic in the dark
And nothing else I'd ever want to do

[Chorus]
So let the night wrap around us tight
While I trace the edges of your skin
All I hear is just our breathing now
This is where the best of us begins

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, it's just our breathing in the dark
[Echo] (Just a steady rhythm and a spark)

[Verse 2]
Moonlight slipping through the window pane
Painting silver lines across your face
I would rather be nowhere at all
If I cannot rest in this embrace

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The room is spinning but I’m holding fast
To the way your fingers pull me near
There's a silent promise in your touch
Telling me exactly what I hear

[Chorus]
So let the night wrap around us tight
While I trace the edges of your skin
All I hear is just our breathing now
This is where the best of us begins

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, it's just our breathing in the dark
[Echo] (Just a steady rhythm and a spark)

[Bridge]
I've wandered down a million miles of road
Just to feel the fire of your touch
Every gentle whisper pulls me close
I have never wanted this so much
I am Saeed, leaving all my walls behind
You are the only heaven on my mind

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (Don't say a word, just let it be)
[Spoken] (You are the only truth I want to see)

[Final Chorus]
So let the night wrap around us tight
While I trace the edges of your skin
All I hear is just our breathing now
This is where the best of us begins
